George R. Williams, Ph.D.

How can I serve you? For almost two decades my life work has been driven by the passion to see the lives of men and fathers transformed for the benefit of their children, families and communities. This passion combined with my education, experience and skills make me an ideal candidate to assist you or your organization's state or national scalable, measurable and sustainable family strengthening planning and programming.

My education as a marriage and family therapist and family studies researcher and my experiences working corporate, non-profit, faith community, consultant and state government leadership positions and my skills as a manager, trainer, presenter, curriculum developer, program implementer and grant writer give me a unique edge to achieving your vision to strengthen families in your sphere of influence. Let's work together!
