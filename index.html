George R. Williams Ph.D. candidate

I am glad you stopped by, whether intentionally or randomly. Please don't rush off. Why not take the time to learn about my background and consulting. My expertise is in family relationships, particularly manhood and fatherhood.

For almost two decades my life work has been driven by the passion to see the lives of men and fathers transformed for the benefit of their children, families and communities. This passion combined with my education, experience and skills make me an ideal candidate to assist you or your organization's state or national scalable, measurable and sustainable family strengthening planning and programming.

My education as a marriage and family therapist and family studies researcher and my experiences working corporate, non-profit, faith community, consultant and state government leadership positions and my skills as a manager, trainer, presenter, curriculum developer, program implementer and grant writer give me a unique edge to achieving your vision to strengthen families in your sphere of influence.

I created this website in Dreamweaver. The menu bar above will help you navigate to areas that may spark your interest. I hope you will be encouraged or inspired with the possible outcome of working together.
