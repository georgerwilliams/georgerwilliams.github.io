Welcome

George R. Williams, Ph.D.

How Can I Serve You?

For almost two decades my life work has been driven by the passion to bring innovative, relevant, engaging, and competency enriching services and resources to helping professionals and organizations who serve families and communities. These services include keynotes, workshops, programming, curriculum development, writing, research and consulting.

Let's work together!

My education as a marriage and family therapist and family studies researcher and my experiences working corporate, not-for-profit, faith community, consultant and state government leadership positions and my skills as a manager, trainer, presenter, curriculum developer, program implementer and grant writer give me a unique edge to achieving your vision to strengthen families in your community.
