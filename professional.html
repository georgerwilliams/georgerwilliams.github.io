My Resume

Dr. George R. Williams has decades of experience in leadership, curricula and program development and training. Here are a few highlights from his work. At the National Center for Fathering he trained over 500 trainers across the country in his Quenching the Father-Thirst: Developing and Dad 12-week fatherhood strengthening program for marginalized men. At the State of Kansas he led, created, implemented and assessed the statewide Strong Dads Super Kids healthy father involvement program and curriculum at 13 Early Head Start programs involving over 1,000 families. Also with the Kansas Department for Children and families he developed, trained and evaluated four highly rated Kansas Welfare Professional Training Programs including a Trauma-Informed Care course and curriculum for 500 social workers for 6 hours of Continuing Education Units.

Trauma-Resiliency Endorsement

Dr. George Williams has put to good use his education and experience as a trained marriage and family therapist and family life education consultant. He has infused trauma-resiliency learning and practice based on the ACE’s study into all his individual and family social helping.

He has done this for nearly 500 social workers in Kansas with his highly rated “Trauma, Drama, and Calm,” “Self-Caring Preventative Attention,” and Trauma-Informed Fathering curricula and trainings and also, with “Somatic Experience Trauma Informed Parenting.”

Dr. George’s masterful presentation skills uses artistry, sincerely and humility to connect with the audience as a guide on the journey of experiential and practical learning.
